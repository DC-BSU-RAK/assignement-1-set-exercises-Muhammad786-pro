Spider-Man (2002)

Peter Parker, a shy high school student, gains superpowers after being bitten by a genetically modified spider. As he grapples with his newfound abilities and personal loss, he transforms into Spider-Man, the masked vigilante fighting crime and facing off against the menacing Green Goblin. With great power comes great responsibility.

Key Information

  • Genre: Action, Superhero, Adventure
  • Directed by: Sam Raimi
  • Written by: David Koepp
  • Starring: Tobey Maguire, Kirsten Dunst, Willem Dafoe
  • Release Year: 2002
  • Production: Columbia Pictures, Marvel Enterprises

Cast & Creative Team

  • Tobey Maguire as Peter Parker / Spider-Man
  • Kirsten Dunst as Mary Jane Watson
  • Willem Dafoe as Norman Osborn / Green Goblin
  • James Franco as Harry Osborn
  • Cliff Robertson as Uncle Ben
  • Rosemary Harris as Aunt May

Main Characters

Peter Parker / Spider-Man: A nerdy, kind-hearted teenager who becomes a superhero after a spider bite grants him amazing powers. He battles inner turmoil and villains while trying to live a normal life.

Norman Osborn / Green Goblin: A wealthy scientist and father of Peter’s best friend. His alter-ego emerges after exposure to an unstable performance enhancer, becoming Spider-Man's first nemesis.

Reviews

"Spider-Man" (2002) received critical acclaim for its exciting action, heartfelt storytelling, and Tobey Maguire’s earnest performance. It was a major box office hit and set a benchmark for modern superhero films.
