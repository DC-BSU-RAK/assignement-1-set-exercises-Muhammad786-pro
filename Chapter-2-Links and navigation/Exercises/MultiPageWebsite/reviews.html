Reviews

Critics praised the film's action sequences, visual effects, and performances, particularly that of Tobey Maguire as Peter Parker. It remains a fan favorite.

Roger Ebert praised the film as “an exciting and emotionally rich superhero origin story.”

Fans loved the balance between high-stakes action and Peter’s personal journey, helping the film achieve cult classic status.
