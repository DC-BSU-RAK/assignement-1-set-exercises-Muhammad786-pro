Characters

Explore the key characters of Spider-Man (2002), including their traits and development throughout the film.

Mary Jane Watson: Peter's longtime crush and aspiring actress. She’s supportive and kind-hearted, but caught in the crossfire of Peter’s double life.

Harry Osborn: Peter's best friend and Norman’s son. He struggles with living in his father's shadow and feels conflicted about Peter's growing bond with Mary Jane.
