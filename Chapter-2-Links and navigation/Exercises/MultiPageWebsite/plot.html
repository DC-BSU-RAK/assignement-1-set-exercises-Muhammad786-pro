Plot Summary

Peter Parker, a shy high school student, gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a genetically modified spider. As he grapples with his powers, the death of his Uncle Ben teaches him that "with great power comes great responsibility." He becomes Spider-Man and must stop the villainous Green Goblin while protecting the ones he loves.

The story also explores Peter's internal struggle between his responsibilities as a hero and his desires as a teenager. His relationships with Mary Jane and Harry Osborn are tested, especially as Norman Osborn's descent into madness threatens everyone Peter loves. The emotional weight of Uncle Ben’s death becomes Peter’s driving force, shaping his identity as Spider-Man.
