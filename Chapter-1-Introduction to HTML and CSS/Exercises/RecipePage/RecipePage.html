Chicken Biryani

A comforting, aromatic rice dish layered with tender marinated chicken, warm spices, and herbs. A soulful favorite for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil or ghee

Method

  1. Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Let it sit for 1 hour.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown and set aside half for layering.
  4. Cook marinated chicken with tomatoes and half of the fried onions until tender.
  5. Boil soaked rice with whole spices until 70% cooked, then drain.
  6. Layer chicken and rice in a pot, add fried onions, herbs, and saffron milk.
  7. Cover and cook on low (dum) for 20â€“25 minutes.
  8. Fluff gently and serve hot with raita or salad.
